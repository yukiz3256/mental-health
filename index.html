What Is Mental Health?
Mental Health is something that includes our emotions, psychology and our social wealth. It is different for everyone, it affects how he/she thinks, acts and feels, therefore can change how someone handles their stress and choices. It's not a easy topic to discuss or bring up about, any can experience mental health problems from child to adulthood. From themselves, to family problems to past life experiences. Most common illnesses are depression, anxiety disorders, Schizophrenia,Eating disorders, and Addictive behaviors.
Symptons
Common symptons are someone feeling sad or down often, getting angry, tired and hungry often, not being able to sleep, not having an appetite, not wanting to talk to family & friends MOSTLY: constant mood changes, sleep & appetite change, loss of interest.
Being Okay
Everyone IS different, and you are not alone. Its okay to feel stressed or exhuasted from school, home, the past or quarantine.
